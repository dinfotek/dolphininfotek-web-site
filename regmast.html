User can immediately print the Share Certificate as well.  Entry of Transfer, Demat, Remat & Buyback of Shares.  Management of ‘Creation of Charges’ including details of modification / satisfaction.  Management of Investments u/s 187(3).  Management of ‘Loans /Guarantees /Securities (u/s 187(9).  Management of Contracts.  Generation of Form DIR-8, DIR-2, Form MBP-1, Letter u/s 184(1).  Generation of the following Statutory registers: o Register of Directors, KMP & their shareholding (u/s 88(1(a)) o Register of Charges (CHG-7). o Register of Investments (MBP-3) o Register of Loans/ Guarantees/ Securities (MBP-2) o Register of Members (MGT-1). o Register of Transfers o Register of Contracts (MBP-4).  Generation of “Annual Return (Form 1)”.  ‘User-defined-report’ facilitates generation of custom reports by allowing the user to select the columns from various tables desired in the report.  One-screen enquiry showing all details of any selected company (or) person.  Concise PDF Snap-shot of any selected company. Regmast Statutory Company Registers Managament System (CA 2013) The highlight of the product is its ‘Quick search’ feature and its ability to generate the forms and statutory registers out of the box.
